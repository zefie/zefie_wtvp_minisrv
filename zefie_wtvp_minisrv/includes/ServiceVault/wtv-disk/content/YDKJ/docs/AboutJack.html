YOU DON'T KNOW JACK is an
irreverent, fast-paced game
where you are the contestant
on a TV Trivia game show. YOU DON'T
KNOW JACK can be played by 1 to 3 players.
The goal of YOU DON'T KNOW JACK is to
rack up play money. If you're the player with
the most play money at the end of the game,
you're the winner.
